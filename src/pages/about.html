We Are Cornell Cup Robotics

Cornell Cup Robotics is a student run organization at Cornell University that designs, manufactures, and creates innovative robotics oriented projects. Over 60 Cornell students work to create dynamic projects that bolster the ingenuity of embedded technologies. The objective of this team is to provide a valuable, practical experience for students with robotics and embedded systems, and to demonstrate the extraordinary technology that we are able to create.

Team Breakdown

Each subteam within Cornell Cup has a team lead. The three main subteams this year are the R2-D2, Minibot, and Modbot projects. But, as always, we are looking to expand and grow our Cornell Cup family to make us perform as efficiently as possible. As such, this year we added a Business team and a small Web Development team.

Dr. Dave Schneider, Advisor
